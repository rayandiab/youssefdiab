Our Story

At Yalla Eat, we’re not just serving chicken — we’re serving energy, flavor, and good vibes for students on the go.

Born in front of the Lebanese University – Faculty of Technology, Yalla Eat was created with one mission: to be the go-to spot for students craving bold, juicy meals without breaking the bank. Whether you're powering through a study session or chilling with friends after class, our food is fast, flavorful, and full of attitude.

From our signature Yalla Box to loaded wraps, saucy wings, and crispy bites — everything we serve is made with love, spice, and a whole lot of student spirit. The vibe? Urban, cozy, and Instagram-ready, with neon lights, comfy seats, and even charging spots for your devices.

So grab it. Eat it. Yalla!
This is more than a meal — it's a lifestyle.
